Welcome to Findaway

Nestled in the green mountains of Vermont, Findaway Getaway is a place to pause, breathe, and rediscover your rhythm. Surround yourself with serene forests, placid streams, and the quiet company of beavers, moose, and friends.

We open our doors on January 16, 2025 with warm nights by the fire and mulled cider.
